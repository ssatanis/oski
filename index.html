Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Oski

Everything you need to know about our AI-powered assessment platform

How accurate is Oski's AI assessment compared to human graders?

Oski demonstrates high alignment with human graders, achieving over 95% accuracy and a Cohen's kappa of 0.88 for post-encounter note grading. This indicates strong agreement and reliability.

What video formats does Oski support for assessments?

Oski supports common video formats including MP4, AVI, and MOV for video uploads.

How long does it take to get assessment results?

Oski dramatically reduces turnaround time from weeks to just days.

Can Oski integrate with our existing learning management system?

Oski is designed for flexible integration; please contact us to discuss your specific LMS and API requirements.

Is student data secure and FERPA compliant?

Yes, Oski adheres to strict data protocols, including HIPAA and FERPA compliance, ensuring the safety and privacy of all student information.
