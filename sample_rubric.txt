Station 1A - Physical Examination Rubric

The student should perform the following examinations:
1. General Inspection (2 points) - Student observes patient's general appearance
2. Heart Auscultation (3 points) - Student listens to heart sounds at appropriate locations  
3. Lung Auscultation (3 points) - Student listens to lung sounds systematically
4. Abdominal Examination (2 points) - Student palpates abdomen appropriately

Total: 10 points
